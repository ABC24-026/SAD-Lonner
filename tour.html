Tour FAQs

What time do doors open?

Doors typically open 1 hour before showtime. VIP package holders gain entry 2 hours before showtime.

Are there age restrictions?

Shows are generally all-ages, but venues may have specific policies. Please check with the venue for details.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person in most cases.
