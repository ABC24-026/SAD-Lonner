About SAD Lonner

The Artist Behind the Music

SAD Lonner emerged in 2020 as a boundary-pushing musical project, blending electronic production with organic instruments to create a unique sonic landscape that defies traditional genre classifications.

Born and raised in Moshupa, Botswana, the artist behind SAD Lonner discovered a passion for music at an early age, learning multiple instruments before venturing into electronic production during college years.

After releasing a series of well-received singles on independent platforms,SAD Lonnersigned with Cosmic Records in 2022, allowing for a broader creative vision and global reach.

The debut album "Celestial Harmonies" received critical acclaim, establishing SAD Lonner as an innovative voice in the contemporary music scene. Now, with the sophomore release "Cosmic Waves," the artistic journey continues to evolve, pushing sonic boundaries while maintaining an emotional core that resonates with listeners worldwide.

SAD Lonner's live performances are known for their immersive visual elements and dynamic energy, creating memorable experiences that extend beyond traditional concerts.
